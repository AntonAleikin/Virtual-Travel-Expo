In 2020, COVID-19 pandemic has affected outbound tourism worldwide. However, 2021 is forecast to see a slight rebound* in international travel.
With this in mind, we would like to invite you to take part in Ukraine’s first ever Virtual Travel Expo, which will be held on 17-19 February 2021.
A virtual expo is an efficient and cost-effective way to bring international tourism players together to discuss a road map to recovery, identify the trends and innovation shaping the future of the industry and reach out to potential buyers.
About 2000 tour agents are expected to visit the event. The exhibitors will be represented by Ukraine‘s leading tour operators as well as international tourism destinations, hotels, and airlines.
In fact, the virtual exhibition is your familiar offline event, only you do not need to think about the physical stand, air tickets, accommodation, business cards, promotional material, etc.
Ukraine Virtual Travel Expo 2021 will be based on a modern virtual event platform where exhibitors can create online events for immersive interaction with attendees.
